Contact Us

We’d love to hear from you! Whether you’re looking to adopt, volunteer, or need assistance with a stray cat, we’re here to help. Below are the ways you can get in touch with us.

Found a Cat?

If you’ve found a lost or stray cat, please reach out to us as soon as possible. We can help guide you through the process of checking for identification, reporting the cat, or finding a safe place for them. Send us an email or give us a call with the following details:

  • Location where the cat was found
  • Description of the cat (colour, size, any distinguishing features)
  • Any immediate concerns (injury, sickness, etc.)

Email: saveafighter@gmail.com

Want to Volunteer?

We’re always looking for passionate cat lovers to join our team! Volunteers play a vital role in helping us care for cats, organize events, and find forever homes for our furry friends. Whether you have a few hours to spare or want to commit to regular volunteering, we’d love to have you on board.

Volunteer Inquiries: helpafighter@gmail.com
