Here’s how the adoption process works

  1. Once you’ve found a cat, you’ll need to fill in a homing questionnaire
  2. We’ll then complete the cat matching process with you. This helps us to ensure that you and your pet are right for each other
  3. You may be given the option to meet your potential pet by making an appointment to visit them in the centre
  4. Living in rented accommodation? You might need a letter from your landlord
  5. You’ll then fill in some paperwork to adopt your cat, pay your adoption fee and arrange a date to collect your new companion
